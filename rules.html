GhostSMP Server Rules

    1. Respect All Players

    Treat everyone with respect. No harassment, insults, or bullying No discrimination (racist, sexist, etc.) No trolling or intentionally annoying behavior No excessive arguing or negativity Respect staff and their decisions No sharing personal information Avoid controversial or heated topics

    1.1 Player Safety

    Adults (18+) may NOT engage in romantic or inappropriate behavior with minors Keep all interactions friendly and game-related Respect personal boundaries

    2. No Encouraging Rule Breaking

    Do not encourage others to break rules Do not promote cheating, exploits, or toxic behavior Do not start or fuel drama

    3. Chat Rules

    Keep chat clean and readable. No spam or flooding No advertising (servers, Discords, etc.) Keep swearing minimal and not directed at others No excessive caps English only in global chat Keep messages appropriate for all ages

    4. No Impersonation

    Do not pretend to be staff Do not use staff-like names or roles Do not act like you have authority

    5. Land & Building Rules

    Do not claim land you won’t use Inactive claims may be removed

    Build Content

    Not Allowed:

    NSFW or inappropriate content Realistic modern weapons Gore, drugs, or hateful symbols

    Allowed:

    Fantasy, creative, and appropriate builds Mild (non-graphic) horror Staff have final say.

    6. Fair Play

    Play fairly at all times.

    6.1 Allowed Mods

    Performance mods (FPS boost) Shaders / visual packs Basic HUD improvements Litematica (visual only)

    6.2 Not Allowed

    Hacks or unfair advantages Auto-building or automation mods

    6.3 Exploits

    No bug abuse or duplication glitches No bypassing server mechanics

    6.4 Server Performance

    No lag machines No chunk loaders No excessive entities or farms

    7. PvP Rules

    No optimizers (PvP Optimizer, Marlowww's Crystal Optimizer, etc.)

    8. Alt Accounts

    One account at a time No using alts for advantages

    9. No Scamming

    No fake or misleading trades No tricking players No unfair auction listings If it’s dishonest, it’s not allowed.

    10. Server Safety

    No threats (DDoS, hacking, etc.) No spreading false information No organizing attacks or drama

    11. Economy & Shops

    No real-world trading No scam shops Shops must be fair and active

    12. Additional Rules

    Keep usernames and skins appropriate Do not bypass bans Do not beg staff for items Do not offer real-life services Do not access restricted areas

    13. Staff Authority

    Staff decisions are final Punishments depend on severity Staff may act on situations not listed Final Note

    Follow the rules, respect others, and enjoy your time on GhostSMP.
